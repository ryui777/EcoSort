Our Mission & Objectives

Promoting sustainable waste management through innovation and community engagement.

  • Design smart bins that auto-segregate waste and monitor fill levels in real-time.
  • Develop data analytics for KPIs (diversion rate, contamination, precision/recall) to inform sustainability decisions.
  • Evaluate system performance through controlled tests focusing on accuracy, efficiency, and reliability.
  • Promote environmental awareness and encourage active participation by students and staff.
  • Enable secure over-the-air updates and remote diagnostics for minimal downtime.
  • Implement predictive pickup scheduling and route optimization to reduce trips by 20%.
